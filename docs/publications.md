## Papers

**DRESS: A Continuous Framework for Structural Graph Refinement**
E. Castrillo. arXiv preprint, 2026.
[arXiv:2602.20833](https://github.com/velicast/dress-graph/blob/main/research/k-DRESS.pdf)

**DRESS and the WL Hierarchy: Climbing One Deletion at a Time**
E. Castrillo. Preprint, 2026.
[vertex-k-DRESS.pdf](https://github.com/velicast/dress-graph/blob/main/research/vertex-k-DRESS.pdf)

Proves unconditionally that Δᵏ-DRESS distinguishes every CFI(K_{k+3}) pair for all k ≥ 0 (CFI Staircase Theorem), via the CFI Deck Separation theorem and Virtual Pebble Lemma. Also proves Δᵏ-DRESS ≥ (k+2)-WL for all graphs conditional on the WL-Deck Separation Conjecture.

**Breaking Hard Isomorphism Benchmarks with DRESS**
E. Castrillo. Preprint, 2026.
[delta1-dress-hard-families.pdf](https://github.com/velicast/dress-graph/blob/main/research/delta1-dress-hard-families.pdf)

Empirical evaluation of Δ¹-DRESS on 51,816 graphs across 34 hard benchmark families (16 SRG families + 18 constructed), achieving 100% separation and resolving over 576 million non-isomorphic pairs. Establishes that Δ¹-DRESS strictly exceeds 3-WL.
